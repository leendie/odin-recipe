Bingeing with Babish Brownies

Babish Brownies

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups white sugar
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1 ¼ cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 4 large eggs plus 1 large egg white
  • ½ cup water
  • 1 ½ cups all purpose flour

Steps

  1. Line a 9x13 inch pan with parchment paper and oil the whole thing down with vegetable oil.
  2. Add sugar and butter to the bowl of a stand mixer. Cream together with the paddle attachment until light and fluffy.
  3. Add cocoa powder, kosher salt, and baking powder in a separate bowl. Whisk to combine, and then add to stand mixer bowl. Combine on slow speed.
  4. Add eggs, one at a time, to the bowl of the stand mixer as it mixes. Wait until each egg is fully incorporated before adding the next one.
  5. Add ½ cup water and beat on high speed until the batter is nice and creamy.
  6. Add the flour to the bowl of the stand mixer and minimally mix. Don’t overmix!
  7. Pour batter into prepared pan and bake at 350°F for 25-30 minutes, until it resembles a cake. Test with a toothpick to ensure that it is cooked.
  8. Use parchment paper to lift the brownies out of the pan and allow them to cool on a wire rack for at least 1 hour.
  9. Optional: Make frosting. In the bowl of a stand mixer, combine softened butter, powdered sugar, cocoa powder, and milk. Mix on high speed for about a minute, or until completely combined.
  10. Top brownies with frosting and optional sprinkles. Serve and enjoy!
